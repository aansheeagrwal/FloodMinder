About FloodML

Empowering communities and authorities to stay ahead of floods with actionable insights.

Floods are among the most destructive and frequent natural disasters worldwide.

Each year, floods claim thousands of lives and displace millions. Regions in India and China's Yellow River Valley often witness some of the most severe floods in history.

FloodML predicts flood events and their potential impact in advance, presenting the information through interactive visualizations that are clear and easy to understand.

Our Services

Tools and insights to help communities stay flood-ready and assist authorities in effective planning and resource allocation.

Flood Plots

Detailed visualizations of flood forecasts, rainfall patterns, and possible damage zones.

Flood Heatmaps

Interactive heatmaps showing live flood conditions across multiple regions.

Satellite Data

Geospatial visualizations of flood-prone areas using NASA's GPM project datasets.

AI Flood Prediction

Forecast upcoming floods in real-time with our advanced machine learning algorithm.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why FloodML?

    Over 80% of India's population, approximately 1.08 billion people, are at risk from floods. They cause nearly $15 billion in losses annually. FloodML provides early warning solutions to mitigate these risks.

  • How it works

    Data Sources: Scraped flood data from floodlist.com and historical weather via Visual Crossing API.

    ML Model: Random Forest classifier with 98.71% accuracy.

    Visualization: Plots generated using Plotly and satellite imagery.

    Frontend: Developed using Flask with responsive HTML/CSS templates.

  • Achievements

    - Built one of India's most comprehensive flood datasets.
    - High-accuracy ML model for prediction.
    - Clean, interactive visualizations for easy interpretation.

FloodML leverages AI and visual analytics to predict floods and visualize potential impact zones effectively.
